How this site was made

Last updated on 2019-11-11 1 min read

This site was made in Rstudio using Hugo, blogdown, git, and netlify. Here are the resources I used when building the site.

I mainly followed Allison Hill’s guide. She also has a number of other posts on blogdown that are helpful.

I occasionally cross referenced Amber Thomas’ guide.

If you need the mile high view before you dive in, read Chi Zhang’s post.

You’ll of course need the official Hugo refence and creating websites with R markdown.

Happy web building!
